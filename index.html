Making Things Happen!

Welcome to my digital portfolio, a reflection of my journey as a Seeker, Explorer, Maker, and Solver.
My name is Hari Sharan Dwivedi,
Rapid prototyping and Digital fabrication are my two great loves. I contribute a fresh viewpoint to the process of discovering and producing useful products as well as solutions thanks to my distinctive combination of schooling in Sanskrit Linguistics and Philosophy.

In my portfolio, you'll discover a complete documentation of my finest work, as well as an honest depiction of my failures and shortcomings. Through my work at AIC NITTE FabLab, you'll see my commitment to excellence and my ability to add value to every project I tackle.

I tackle challenges with eagerness and aim to create impactful solutions. Pursuing excellence is a lifelong journey and I look forward to sharing my work, receiving feedback, and exploring our collective future together.
